Ikana Valley is in the eastern part of Termina. It is cursed and deserted. It is only home to the dead and spirits.
Road to Ikana The Road to Ikana is a rocky gorge ridden with iron fences and ancient statues. It leads to Ikana Graveyard and the main canyon.
Ikana Graveyard Ikana Graveyard is burial ground run and patrolled by Dampe.
Music Box House The Music Box House is where Pamela and her father live. They fight off the undead with the song "Farewell to Gibdos" which plays from the instrument above their house.
