Main Characters

LINK
Link is the main character. He is the main character in all the Legend of Zelda games. The heros of the Legend of Zelda games all share the name Link. These Links might have been the same person, a series of familial descendents, or a number of heros with different names entirely. The Links of certain eras may also have been named after the legendary hero. This Link set off on his horse, Epona, who lived at Lon Lon Ranch. Months passed as he wondered in search of his companion, Navi, eventually losing himself in a mysterious forest. It just so happened that this forest led to a parallel world known as Termina. Only Child Link appears in Majora's Mask. Link takes on many forms in this series through different masks.
SKULL KID
There are many skull kids. Skull kids are the combination of lost children's souls who get lost in the Forbidden Forest. This particular Skull Kid is the main villan of this game. He finds friends in two faries, Tael and Tatl, and create a lot of mischief together. Skull Kid steals the mask from the Mask Salesman to have power. He uses the mask's power to create chaos in the parallel world, where he forces the moon to crash into the earth.
TATL
A fairy who, together with her brother, Tael, makes mischief with Skull Kid. She is left behind by the imp while trying to escape from Link, and eventually she joins forces with the Hero.
TAEL
Tael is a fairy who, together with his sister, Tatl, makes mischief with Skull Kid. He stays with Skull Kid when they get separated from Tatl.
MASK SALESMAN
He travels through different lands searching for masks. He is wondering through the Forbidden Forest when he is stopped by Skull Kid. He reveals to Skull Kid the power of Majora's Mask by mistake. Skull Kid steals the mask from the Happy Mask Salesman. He follows Link and reveals to him what has happened and that Link has to retrieve the mask and save the world.
MAJORA'S MASK
Majora's Mask is the primary antagonist of the game. It was used by an Ancient Tribe as a form of hexing and torture. The maskis primarily seen being worn by Skull Kid, who uses it's dark magic to wreak havoc across Termina. He is also a sinister mask that was used for incantations long ago.
THE FOUR GIANTS
The Four Giants are the Legendary Giants. They are guardian deities that inhabit the region of Termina. They each inhabit the four corners of the earth.

Clock Town Characters

Bombers Secret Society of Justice
The Bombers Secret Society of Justice is a group of young boys who solve the problems and mysteries of the other citizens of Clock Town. They have their own base in Clock Town next to the mayor's office. They are scattered all around town in the northern, western an eastern areas to help others; however,they are not in the south part of town. They wear distinct bandanas. Jim is the leader, who wears a red banada, while the others wear a blue bandana. They also have numbers on thebacks of their shirts to distinguish them. They have their own rules and carry around a notepad, which they give to link when he joins, where they write down everything that goes on in town.

Characters Found Everywhere

Tingle
Tingle is a recurring character in the series. He sells self-made maps of Termina and regions in Termina. He can be found in North Clock Town, Southern Swamp., Milk Road, Goron Village, Great Bay Coast, and Ikana Canyon. He loves forest fairies and wants to be one himself.
Epona
Epona is Link's horse. Link can call her to anywhere he is by playing Epona's Song on his ocarina.
