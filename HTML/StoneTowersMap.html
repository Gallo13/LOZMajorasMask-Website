The Stone Towers is located at the far end of the Ikana Canyon. It stands east of the abandoned kingdom of Ikana. Stone Tower is the largest crafted object in Termina. It's height is surpassed only by the mountain of Snowhead. It is much taller than Clock Tower. It has countless holes on the surface, which could be windws through which boulders fall. The interior is a huge vertical shaft with floating blocks, which move when a certain switch is pressed. This area of Termina is unlike the other areas of Termina. It is experiencing a dark age because of the rise of spectra, undead creatures and other beasts.
Stone Tower Temple Stone Tower Temple is located at the top of the Stone Towers. In contrast to the area surrounding Stone Towers, the Stone Temple does not show the depressing atmosphere like the outside conditions. Instead, it is a light-themed dungeon that features a potpourri of other elements, including fire, water, and even sky.
