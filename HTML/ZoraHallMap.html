Zora Hall is the home of the Zora race. It is located inside a coral reef a few meters from the river in Zora Cape in Great Bay. Zora Hall consists of vast caverns filled with pools and waterfalls. The entire complex is subterranean. It is also home to the popular Zora Band, The Indigo-Go's. There are many chambers inside Zora Hall, including a room for each of the band members.
