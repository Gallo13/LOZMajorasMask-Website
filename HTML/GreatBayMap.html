Great Bay is a region in Termina that contains the Great Bay Coast and Zora Hall Coast. Great Bay is a tropical region, filled with palm trees, sand, and many creatures. It is home to the Zoras and many unnatural water creatures. It used to be an enchanting sea paradise, but the waters are now contaminated.

Great Bay Coast Great Bay Coast the main part of the region. The coast provides access to the other parts of the western region. It is located directly west of Termina Field.
Marine Research Laboratory The Marine Research Lab is located right by the Great Bay Coast. This is where marine biologists do their experiments. It specializes in the research of water quality and marine life-forms. The Professor works here, researching the creatures of the Great Bay region.
Fisherman's Hut The Fisherman's Hut is where the Fisherman lives. The hut is on the Great Bay Coast.
Oceanside Spider House The Oceanside Spider House is am abandoned house on the Great Bay Coast that is home to regular Skultulas and, most importantly, Gold Skultulas.
Pirates' Fortress Outside of the Pirates' Fortress is Pirates' Cove where a few guards circle the area in motor boats. The Pirates' Cove region is located in northern Great Bay Coast. It is the fortress of the female Gerudo Pirates.
Pinnacle Rock Pinnacle Rock is in the Great Bay Coast. It houses the lair of the Deep Pythons, which consists of a deep hole in the sea filled with predators, all o them sea serpents.
Zora Cape Zora Capeis found through a cave in the southern part of the Great Bay Coast. It is home to many places such as Zora Hall, a Fairy Fountain, the Waterfall Rapids, and a Fishing Hole.
Zora Hall Zora Hall is the home of the Zora race. It is located in Zora Cape in Great Bay. Zora Hall consists of vast caverns filled with pools and waterfalls. The entire complex is subterranean. It is also home to the popular Zora Band, The Indigo-Go's.
Great Bay Temple The Great Bay Temple is inaccessible to most people and is surrounded by a very powerful cyclone of water. It can be seen far away form the Great Bay ast and Zora Cape. The Temple is victim to a curse at leaves the sea of Great Bay murky.
Waterfall Rapids The Waterfall Rapids can be found high above the ground at the southern part of Zora Cape. It consists of several freshwater streams running though narrow channels at high currents. The river flows into a few subterranean caves and gullies, and is riddled with algae and obstructed by rock formations protruding form its bed as well as a large number of roots sprawling from trees on all sides.
