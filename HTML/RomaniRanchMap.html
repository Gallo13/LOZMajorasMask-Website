Romani Ranch is located in south-west Termina. It is famous for its Chateau Romani. It is owned by Cremia. The only way to reach Romani Ranch is through Milk Road, which is located east of the Southern Swamp. It gets it's name because Romani Ranch is known for their milk production and the milk gets transported via this road.
Mama's House Mama's House is where Romani and Cremia live. It is the largest building on the Ranch.
Barn
The Barn is where Cremia keeps the livestock.
Cucco Shack
The Cucco Shack has 10 Cucco chickens in it.
Doggy Racetrack The Doggy Racetrack is home to Mamamu Yan. Link can come here to watch and bet on dog races.
